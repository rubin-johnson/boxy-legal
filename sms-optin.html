Boxy — SMS Opt-In Process

Last updated: May 15, 2026

This page documents how Rubin Johnson, the sole operator and recipient of the Boxy system, opted in to receive SMS messages from the Boxy toll-free number (+1-855-410-4957).

How Opt-In Occurred

Rubin Johnson configured the Boxy system by entering their personal mobile phone number in the LIGHTPHONE_NUMBER environment variable on the Boxy server. After adding the number, Rubin Johnson texted the keyword START from their personal phone to the Boxy toll-free number (+1-855-410-4957) to confirm consent to receive messages.

No third parties are enrolled in this program. This is a closed, single-recipient system operated by and for Rubin Johnson.

Opt-In Confirmation Message

Upon texting START, the Boxy system sent the following confirmation message:

Boxy: You're subscribed. Msg&data rates may apply. ~10 msgs/day. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel.

How to Opt Out

Text STOP to +1-855-410-4957 at any time. After sending STOP, one final confirmation message will be sent and no further messages will be delivered. To re-enroll, text START.

Help

Text HELP to +1-855-410-4957 for support information. You may also contact: rubin.lewis.johnson@gmail.com

Message Frequency and Rates

Approximately 10 messages per day. Message and data rates may apply.
